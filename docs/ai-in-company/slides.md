## Slide 9 — What worked / lessons learned

**What We Learned**

- The biggest value often came from repetitive workflows
- AI works best when embedded into real processes
- Fragmented information creates strong opportunities for AI
- Simple use cases build trust and adoption
- The highest leverage comes from integrations
- AI becomes much more valuable when it can act, not just answer

---

## Slide 10 — Closing / advice to others

**How I’d Approach It Again**

- Start with small repetitive workflows
- Build trust with visible quick wins
- Add knowledge retrieval across fragmented content
- Move into cross-system integrations
- Focus on workflows tied to hiring, sales, or revenue

**AI adoption becomes real when it shifts from demos to operating systems**
